Test cases GammaCiv

UnitActions:
v   * Unit actions can only be done by the player owning the unit.

Settler:
v   * The settler's action removes the settler
v   * The settler's action adds a city at settler's position
v   * The settler's action adds a city at settler's position of same owner as the settler was

Archer:
v   * The archer's action (fortify) doubles the archers defensive strength.
v   * The archer cannot be moved when it's action is active (toggleable).
v   * The archer's action can be toggled off, by using action again, and once
done, it's defensive strength is returned to the original.
